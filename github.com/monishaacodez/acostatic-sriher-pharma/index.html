    Sri Ramachandra Faculty of Pharmacy

    About Us

    Established in 1993, this constituent college is a prestigious institution located in a multilevel building with well-equipped research laboratories and draws research scholars from different parts of the nation. The college offers a 4 year professional degree in pharmacy (B. Pharm.) which teaches pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, pharmaceutics and chemistry of natural products as part of the course. The college also offers a 6-year Pharm. D. course and Pharm.D (Post Baccalaureate) course for those with B.Pharm qualification.

    Most prominent in the course are hospital / clinical pharmacy, health management and research. State of the art equipment and up to date information on the latest drugs and therapies equip the students to practice anywhere in the world. The Undergraduate & Postgraduate degree programs are approved by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi.
